# PMD: Shift!

Welcome to **PMD: Shift!**, an experimental proof-of-concept fan project, intended to explore the world of Pokemon Mystery Dungeon (PMD) as a community developed multiplayer online game.

## Get Started

Instructions to play the game are on our wiki. Follow the links below for more information.

* [Geting Started](https://pmdshift.fandom.com/wiki/Getting_Started)
* [Discord](https://discord.gg/vF5x55N)


## Contributing

Community contributions are managed here. We encourage and welcome all contributions!

You may contribute the following:
* [Graphics](https://github.com/PMDShift/GFX)
* [Skins](https://github.com/PMDShift/Skins)
* [Sound Effects](https://github.com/PMDShift/Sounds)
* Maps
* Dungeons
* Stories and Quests
* ...and more!
